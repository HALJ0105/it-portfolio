Ticketing System Lab

A simulated help desk ticket queue with SLA-based priority tiers and escalation paths, tied back to issues resolved in the Help Desk Lab. In progress.

JIRA Service Management SLA Management Knowledge Base

Planned Scope

  • Set up ticket categories, priority levels, and SLA rules in a JIRA Service Management instance.
  • Write knowledge base articles for common resolutions.
  • Simulate a queue of 15–20 tickets across varying severities and work through triage/escalation logic.
  • Tie simulated tickets to real issues from the Help Desk Lab (AD lockouts, GPO conflicts, connectivity) for an end-to-end workflow.
